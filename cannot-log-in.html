Cannot Log In

If you're unable to log in, please try the following steps:

Steps to Try:

  • Check your credentials: Make sure your email or phone number and password are entered correctly.
  • Forgot Password? Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen to reset your password.
  • Verify account status: Ensure your account is active and not suspended.
  • Clear cache: Clear your app cache or try logging in from a different device.

Contact Support

If you're still having trouble logging in, please contact our support team for assistance:

Email: contact@sportfleks.com

We are here to help you get back into your account as quickly as possible.
