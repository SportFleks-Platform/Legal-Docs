Privacy Policy

Welcome to SportFleks! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and platform, SportFleks.

1. Information We Collect

The application may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to your device type, unique device ID, IP address, operating system, browser type, and details about how you use the app.

a. Personal Information

  • Registration Information: Name, email address, username, password, and profile picture when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Bio, location, and interests you may add to your profile.
  • Contact Information: If you connect social media accounts, we may collect data from those networks.

b. Event Information

  • Event Details: Information about sports events you create, join, or manage, including event name, date, location, and participant details.

c. Usage Data

  • Interaction Data: Features used, settings chosen, and content shared.
  • Device Information: IP address, device ID, operating system.
  • Log Data: Access times, pages viewed, and app usage logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services.
  • Managing your account and personalizing your experience.
  • Facilitating social interactions and event management.
  • Communicating with you (updates, notifications, promotions).
  • Improving the app and developing new features.
  • Ensuring legal compliance and protecting our rights.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate (hosting, data analysis, support).
  • Third Parties for Commercial Purposes: We may share your information with third parties for commercial purposes, including selling your data. By creating an account and using our app, you consent to this data sharing practice.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal data within the app.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@sportfleks.com.

6. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of the app after any changes signifies your acceptance of the new policy.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 1 August 2024.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@sportfleks.com.

By creating an account and using SportFleks, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
