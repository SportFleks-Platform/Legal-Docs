Forgot your password? No problem!

How to Reset Your Password:

If you’re not logged in, simply follow these steps:

  • Tap “Log In”.
  • Select “Phone No or Email”.
  • Tap “Forgot Password?” to begin the password reset process.
  • Choose whether you want to reset your password using your email or phone number.

If you encounter any issues while resetting your password, please contact our support team at: contact@sportfleks.com.

We’re here to help you get back into your account quickly and securely!
